Leo Pledge

I pledge of my hand
extended and open
to help those in need.
I pledge my heart reach for it
and it will be touched.
I pledge of my ears
to hear another’s outcry.
My eyes to see
the plight of other.
My knowledge to bring a man
closer to his dreams.
I pledge of myself
For the betterment of
my communities, my states and
my country.
